Section 06 — Honest answers

Questions you'd ask
at the chai stall.

Will it work on my old Android phone?
Yes.ISP Billing is built as a lightweight progressive web app — no Play Store, no 200 MB download. Works on any phone with Chrome from 2018 onwards. Even on patchy 2G it caches data so you can keep collecting.
Can my field boy see my full customer list and run away with it?
No. Field-staff accounts only see customers assigned to them, can only mark payments as pending, and cannot export data. The owner login is the only one with full access and verification rights.
Do you charge per WhatsApp reminder?
No. Reminders open in your normal WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing the customer's dues and payment link. You send from your own number. Zero per-message charge from us, ever.
What if I want to switch back to Excel later?
One-click export of every customer, payment, and ledger to CSV. Your data is yours. We charge for the software, not for letting you leave.
Do you support languages other than English?
The current build is English-first with Hindi UI strings rolling out this quarter. Customer-facing PDFs and WhatsApp reminders can already be set in Hindi, Marathi, Kannada, Tamil and Telugu.
Is my customer data safe?
Data is hosted on Indian servers, encrypted at rest, and backed up nightly. Each tenant (each ISP) has fully isolated data — your customer list never touches another operator's database.
